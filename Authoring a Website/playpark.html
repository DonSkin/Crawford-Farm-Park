Playpark

Crawford Farm Park's playpark is a fun and safe area for children to play and explore.

The playpark is designed to cater to children of all ages, with a range of equipment from swings, slides, climbing frames and more!

Surroundings

The park is surrounded by beautiful countryside, making it a scenic and peaceful place for families to relax.

Seating Areas

Parents can relax in the nearby seating area while their children play, or join in on the fun themselves.


With a range of exciting equipment, including swings, slides and climbing frames, kids can let their imaginations soar as they explore and play in a safe environment.
