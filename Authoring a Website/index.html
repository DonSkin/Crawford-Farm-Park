Welcome to Crawford Farm Park!

Our park offers a range of activities for visitors of all ages, making it the perfect place for a fun-filled day out with family and friends!

We look forward to welcoming you to the park, where you can enjoy a taste of rural Scotland and create unforgettable memories with your loved ones.

Petting Zoo

Animal lovers will enjoy visiting our children's petting zoo, where they can get up close and personal with a variety of friendly farm animals.

Cafe

For those seeking a tasty bite to eat, our onsite cafe serves up delicious meals, snacks and drinks made with locally-sourced ingredients.

Walks

Our walking trails offer visitors the chance to explore the stunning countryside surrounding Crawford Farm Park.
