Welcome to Crawford Farm Park, an idyllic countryside destination located between Edinburgh and Carlyle!

Our park offers a range of activities for visitors of all ages, making it the perfect place for a fun-filled day out with family and friends.

For those seeking a tasty bite to eat, our onsite cafe serves up delicious meals, snacks and drinks made with locally-sourced ingredients. Our menu is filled with tempting treats from classic Scottish dishes to homemade cakes and sandwiches.

Animal lovers will enjoy visiting our children's petting zoo, where they can get up close and personal with a variety of friendly farm anials. Our experiences staff are always on hand to answer any questions and provide educational information about the animals.

Children can also run, jump and play to their heart's content in our well-equipped playpark, featuring swings, slides and climbing frames.

Our walking trails offer visitors the chance to explore the stunning countryside surrounding Crawford Farm Park. Whether you're looking for a leisurely stroll or a more challenging hike, our trails cater to all levels of fitness and ability.

Before you leave, be sure to visit our shop, which stocks a wide range of local produce grown by the farm. From fresh fruits and vegetables to homemade preserves and baked goods, our shop has something for everyone.

We look forward to welcoming you to Crawford Farm Park, where you can enjoy a taste of rural Scotland and create unforgettable memories with your loved ones.
