The shop at Crawford Farm Park is a treasure trove of local produce and plants. Visitors can browse through a selection of freshly harvested fruits and vegetables, homemade preserves, and baked goods. 

The shop also stocks a variety of plants, including herbs, flowers and shrubs that are grown on the farm.

Additionally, visitors can find unique gifts and souvenirs that celebrate the local area.

The friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to answer questions and help visitors choose the perfect item to take home.
