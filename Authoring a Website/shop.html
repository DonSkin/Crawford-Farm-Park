Shop

The shop at Crawford Farm Park is a treasure trove of local produce and plants.

Visitors can browse through a selection of freshly harvested fruits and vegetables, homemade preserves, and baked goods.

Local Produce

Our shop showcases an array of locally sourced, farm-fresh produce. From crisp, seasonal fruits and vegetables to our homemade preserves.

Gifts and Souvenirs

The shop stocks a variety of plants, including herbs, flowers, and shrubs that are grown on the farm. Additionally the farm stocks gifts and souvenirs that celebrate the local area.


Friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to answer questions and help visitors choose the perfect items.
